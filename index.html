My Projects

Check out my projects throughout my study

Data Science Salaries Analysis and Visualization

This project analyzes and visualizes data science salaries from 2020 to 2022. It provides insights into average salaries based on experience levels, employment types, distribution of company sizes and locations, top employee residence locations, and a geographic visualization of average salaries by country.

Gross Domestic Product (GDP) of Indonesia

The objective of this project is to analyze and present data related to the Gross Domestic Product (GDP) of Indonesia at the provincial level. The project aims to provide insights into the distribution of GDP across provinces and identify the top provinces with the highest GDP in billion Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).

London Bike Rides

This project focuses on analyzing and visualizing data related to bike rides in London during a specific time period. The project aims to provide insights into bike ride patterns and the impact of weather conditions on bike usage.

KNN and K-means Clustering for Data Science Salary Analysis

The goal of this project is to analyze data science salaries using K-nearest neighbors (KNN) and K-means clustering. By applying these algorithms, we aim to gain insights into the factors that influence salary levels
